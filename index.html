THE STORE

Located in the heart of Kreuzberg, our record store is more than just a shop—it's a cultural hub where Berlin's underground music scene comes alive. We celebrate electronic music heritage with a carefully curated selection that blends raw edge with sophisticated design.

By day, we're your go-to destination for rare finds and underground releases. By night, we transform into an intimate event space hosting DJ sets and listening sessions that capture the essence of Berlin's legendary nightlife.
